Privacy Policy

TypeDrop X — Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

TypeDrop X does not collect any personally identifiable information. All game data, including high scores, total letters caught, best combos, and accuracy statistics, is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and never transmitted to any server.

2. Data Storage

Your game progress and high scores are stored exclusively on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data. We have no access to this data at any time.

3. Third-Party Services

TypeDrop X does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

4. Permissions

The app only requires internet permission for the WebView used to display this Privacy Policy page. No other device permissions are requested or used.

5. Children's Privacy

TypeDrop X is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children or any other users, as the app has no data collection mechanisms.
